❓ How to Play

🎯 Your goal: explore the cities, collect gems, and get rich enough to buy a house you can live in — and an airplane to fly the world!

  1. 🕹️ Move with the joystick (bottom-left) or arrow keys. Tap JUMP to hop.
  2. 💎 Collect gems — they're scattered everywhere. Each gem gives you 💰 10.
  3. 🛒 Spend money in the Shop: a 🚲 bike & 🚗 car make you faster, a 🏠 house is your home, an ✈️ airplane is the coolest of all.
  4. 🏠 Bought a house? A golden HOME beam appears in the city. Walk to the door and tap ENTER to go inside and hang out!
  5. ✈️ Travel to Los Angeles, London & Miami. Your money, gems and house come with you.
  6. 👯 Play with a friend from the main menu to explore together.
